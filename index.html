2ND FULL-LENGTH ALBUM

WINGS

BTS will release their second studio album, WINGS.

Having met their most beautiful moment in life through the
album series of the same title, BTS now stands before a
new door. Set for release on October 10, WINGS contains
songs about boys who encounter temptation for the first time
and must ponder and agonize in the face of it. As the seven
boys experience pain and bliss, they bring forth the image of
birds that break out of their shells and try to take flight for the
first time.
